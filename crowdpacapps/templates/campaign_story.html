Campaign title tips

Your campaign title is the first thing donors see. It should be focused, clear, and grab attention. Don't use generic phrases or repeat your name and race.

"Marine Veteran to Reform the Veterans Administration"
"We need a biology teacher to take on Big Oil"

"Freedom for All"
"James Jones for DPS School Board"

Tips for telling your story

In 3-6 paragraphs, tell your campaign’s story. Answer questions like “Why are you running?” and “Why should someone contribute to your campaign?” Keep it personal and specific.
