On Crowdpac, donors pay the credit card processing fee so you keep more than ever before.

Crowdpac is a free platform with zero startup or monthly costs. We offer two types of fundraising campaigns: conditional and direct donation.

Conditional campaigns

Conditional campaigns collect pledges before hitting a goal, or before a candidate enters a race. They are free to set up. There is a 2.90% + 20 cents for credit card processing fees and ACH is FREE that is charged if, and only if, the pledges are converted to a contribution. Campaigns have a flat 3% fee on contributions, which is the lowest fee in online fundraising.

Direct donate campaigns

Direct donate campaigns on Crowdpac have the lowest fees in online political fundraising. Contributors pay the credit card processing fees (2.9% + 20 cents) or use ACH for free. Campaigns have a flat 3% fee on contributions, which is the lowest fee in online fundraising.
